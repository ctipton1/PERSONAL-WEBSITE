About

Hi, my name is Chance Tipton. I am an aspiring investor, small business owner, and finance/tech enthusiast. I recently graduated from Georgia College & State University with a BBA in Management Information Systems, where I developed a strong foundation in technology, data, and problem-solving.

Beyond my career interests, I enjoy spending time outdoors, hunting, fishing, and exploring new places to keep me grounded and balanced. I’m also deeply interested in personal finance and long-term wealth building, especially through real estate and the stock market.

What drives me most is the idea of building something lasting—whether that’s a business, a property portfolio, or a new project that helps others. I value continuous learning, discipline, and taking on challenges that push me forward both professionally and personally.
